h2. R-Project is Open Source

R is licensed under the Free Software Foundation's GNU General Public License, which means you are free to use it for commercial or non-commercial purposes. You are encouraged to modify it further to suit your needs. There are no licensing fees associated with the use of R. There is nothing to buy and there are no hidden costs. Our users are all running the same version of the software, so there is no special <i>"community edition"</i> with less features.
